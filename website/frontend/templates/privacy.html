1. Introduction

Nexus ("we", "us", "our") operates the Nexus website and application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you sign in with Google OAuth, we collect:

  • Google Account Email - For account identification and communication
  • Google Account Name - Displayed in your profile
  • Google ID - Used internally to link your account

2.2 YouTube Channel Data

With your authorization, we collect publicly available YouTube data:

  • Channel name, description, ID, and URL
  • Subscriber count (daily snapshots)
  • Total video views and video count
  • Channel avatar/thumbnail
  • Stream metadata and timestamps

2.3 What We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect: your YouTube password, private messages or DMs, chat messages from your stream, viewer personal information, credit card information, or browsing history outside Nexus.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide the Service - Display your channel data and analytics
  • Operate the Bot - Post messages according to your configuration
  • Improve Platform - Understand how users interact with Nexus
  • Technical Support - Debug issues and provide support
  • Security - Detect and prevent unauthorized access
  • Communication - Send important updates about the Service

4. Data Storage and Retention

Analytics Data: We store up to 24 months of historical channel data. Data older than 24 months is automatically deleted.

Account Data: Retained while your account is active. You can request deletion anytime.

Backups: Secure backups for disaster recovery. Deleted data may exist in backups for up to 30 days.

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT sell your data.

We do not share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Google - Via OAuth for authentication
  • YouTube - To post messages as your bot (with your authorization)
  • Service Providers - Hosting providers under strict NDAs
  • Legal Requirements - When required by law

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

Depending on your location, you have the right to:

  • Access - Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction - Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion - Request deletion of your data
  • Portability - Request data in machine-readable format
  • Opt-out - Opt out of data processing
  • Withdraw Consent - Withdraw authorization anytime

To exercise these rights, reach out through our contact page or Discord server.

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • HTTPS/SSL encryption for all communications
  • OAuth 2.0 authentication (no stored passwords)
  • Regular security audits
  • Restricted database access
  • Encrypted backups

8. Cookies

We use cookies to maintain your session, remember preferences, and analyze usage. You can control cookies in your browser settings.

9. Children's Privacy

Nexus is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware a child has provided information, we will delete it immediately.

10. Data Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data:

  1. Log into your Nexus account
  2. Go to Settings and select Account
  3. Click "Delete Account" and confirm

Your data will be removed within 30 days. You can also reach out through our contact page or Discord server to request deletion.

11. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy:

Reach out through our contact page or Discord server.
