What you get

Live Chat Bot

It runs as nexusbetabot in your YouTube live chat. Spam gets handled, commands get answered, timed messages go out — you just stream.

Stream Analytics

Viewer count, chat activity, sub changes — tracked over time. No fluff, just the numbers that matter to you.

Custom Commands

!discord, !socials, whatever you want. Create commands from the dashboard and the bot handles the rest in chat.

Google OAuth Login

Sign in with your Google account. Nexus never sees your password and you can revoke access anytime from your Google settings.

Webhooks

Going live? Hit a milestone? Something happens in chat? Nexus can ping Discord or any webhook URL you set up.

Simple Dashboard

One page, everything you need. Bot toggle, stats, commands — it's all there without buried menus or confusing navigation.

Setup takes about 5 minutes

1

Sign in with Google

Click the button, pick your Google account. That's it for this step.

2

Pick your channel

Nexus finds your YouTube channel automatically. If you have multiple, just pick which one.

3

Add nexusbetabot as mod

YouTube Studio → Settings → Community → Moderators. Add nexusbetabot.

4

Turn the bot on

Flip the switch on your dashboard. The bot joins your next live chat.
