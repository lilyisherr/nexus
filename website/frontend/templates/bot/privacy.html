Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what data Nexus Bot collects, how it is used, and how you can control it. I take your privacy seriously and only collect what is necessary to provide the service.

1. What I Collect

Data Why Source
Discord user ID Identify your account Discord OAuth
Discord username and display name Show your name in the dashboard Discord OAuth
Discord avatar Show your profile picture Discord OAuth
Server list (servers you manage) Show which servers you can configure Discord OAuth (guilds scope)
Server configurations Store your bot settings per server You (via dashboard)
Moderation logs Record mod actions for review Bot activity in your server

2. What I Do Not Collect

  • Message content (the bot processes messages in real-time for moderation but does not store them)
  • Direct messages
  • Voice channel audio
  • Email addresses (unless you link a Nexus account)
  • Payment information (there is nothing to pay for)

3. How Data Is Used

All collected data is used exclusively to provide and improve the bot service. Specifically:

  • Your Discord information is used to authenticate you and display your profile
  • Server configurations are used to determine how the bot behaves in your server
  • Moderation logs are stored so you can review actions taken by the bot and your moderators

4. Data Sharing

I do not sell, share, or give your data to any third party. Data is only shared with:

  • Discord (as part of normal bot operation via the Discord API)
  • The database hosting provider (to store your data securely)

5. Data Storage

Data is stored in a PostgreSQL database. OAuth tokens are stored securely and used only to maintain your login session and fetch your server list.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data is kept as long as your account exists
  • Server configurations are kept as long as your account exists
  • Moderation logs are kept indefinitely unless you delete your account
  • When you delete your account, all associated data is permanently removed

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • View all data associated with your account through the dashboard
  • Delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Account page
  • Unlink your Nexus account at any time
  • Remove the bot from your server at any time through Discord

8. Cookies

The dashboard uses session cookies to keep you logged in. No tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or advertising cookies are used.

9. Children's Privacy

Nexus Bot is not intended for users under 13. If you are under 13, do not use the service. This aligns with Discord's own age requirement.

10. Changes

I may update this Privacy Policy when new features are added or data practices change. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the most recent version.

11. Contact

For privacy-related questions or data requests, reach out through the Nexus community Discord server or at nexusbeta.vercel.app/contact.
