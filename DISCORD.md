# Nexus Discord Server

Join the official Nexus Discord community where creators connect, share ideas, and get support.

## Join Us

**Invite Link:** [https://discord.gg/mzxHumP5nR](https://discord.gg/mzxHumP5nR)

## What the Server Offers

- **Announcements** — Be the first to hear about new features, updates, and changes to Nexus before they hit the blog.
- **Support** — Get help directly from the developer and other community members. No waiting for email replies.
- **Feature Requests** — Suggest features and vote on what gets built next. Some of the best Nexus features came from community ideas.
- **Bug Reports** — Found something broken? Report it and help make Nexus better for everyone.
- **General Chat** — Talk shop with other streamers and content creators who use Nexus.
- **Beta Testing** — Get early access to new features and help test them before they go live.
- **Dev Logs** — Follow along with development progress and see what's being worked on in real time.

## Server Rules

1. **Be respectful** — Treat everyone with courtesy. No harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.
2. **Stay on topic** — Use the appropriate channels for your discussions.
3. **No spam** — Avoid excessive self-promotion, repeated messages, or unsolicited advertisements.
4. **No NSFW content** — Keep all content safe for work and appropriate for all ages.
5. **Listen to moderators** — Follow instructions from server staff. Their decisions are final.
6. **No sharing of personal information** — Protect your privacy and respect others' privacy.
7. **English only** — To keep communication accessible, please use English in all channels.
8. **Have fun** — This is a community for creators. Enjoy it.

## Who Is This For?

- YouTube streamers and content creators using Nexus
- Anyone interested in trying out the Nexus platform
- Developers and beta testers who want to help improve Nexus
- Anyone looking to connect with like-minded creators

If you have any questions before joining, reach out through the [contact page](https://nexusbeta.vercel.app/contact) on the Nexus website.
